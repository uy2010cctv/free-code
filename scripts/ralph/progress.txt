## Codebase Patterns
- SessionManager stores sessions in `.free-code-sessions/` directory with `index.json` and individual `#{id}.json` files
- Session interface is defined in `src/web/engine/types.ts`
- SessionManager is initialized in `server.ts` and manages all session lifecycle
- Enterprise session state fields (selectedConnectorIds, selectedModuleIds, lastReportId) are initialized as empty arrays/undefined in createSession()
- Use `saveSession(sessionId)` to persist after any modification
